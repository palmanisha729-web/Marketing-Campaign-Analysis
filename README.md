📊 Project Overview
Business Problem: Marketing teams struggle with budget inefficiency – 38.34% campaigns overspent, unclear channel ROI, and suboptimal product-channel combinations.

Objective: Analyze 100K campaigns (2022-2025) to identify top-performing channels/products and deliver actionable recommendations for 25%+ ROI improvement.

Key Results:

$3.5B Revenue generated

92.96% Average ROI

Email = Top revenue channel

Billboard = Best ROI (93.97%)

March = Peak performance month
